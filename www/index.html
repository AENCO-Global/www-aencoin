WHAT IS AEN TOKEN?

AEN Token Fuels and Facilitates an Ecosystem for HealthTech Entrepreneurialism, Innovation, and Research

As a one-stop-shop solutions provider and research collaboration hub for healthcare companies involved in blockchain development, Aenco combines it solutions platform and core financial ecosystem through the release of the AEN token, as the key building block and extensions across all service and community deliveries.
