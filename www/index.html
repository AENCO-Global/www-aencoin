Frequently Asked Questions

We have provided answers to common questions we have been asked below.

1 What is Aenco?

Aenco is a "one stop shop" for the delivery of cutting edge investment banking, prime brokerage and core banking solutions for the HealthTech industry, as well as a research and collaboration hub for healthcare companies and projects involved in the field of blockchain development.

Aenco will be combining its solutions platform and core banking ecosystem with the wider healthcare technology community, through the AEN token, as the key ecosystem building block and extensions across all its service and community deliveries.

Check out our Whitepaper for more information.

2 Do you accept other methods of contribution such as credit card?

No. Bitcoin (BTC) and Ethereum (ETH) are the only accepted method of contribution as part of the token sale.

3 Can I / How do I participate in the token sale?

The token sale is currently on private sale phase.

We are not accepting contributions from USA, China, and OFAC sanctioned countries. For Hong Kong based contributors, you must qualify and provide wealth proof as a "Professional Investor" under the Hong Kong SFC Guidance and Rules.

4 What are the requirements to participate in the token sale?

We require you to go through our Sign Up process. To acquire AEN tokens, we also require you to go through our KYC Verfication.

5 Will you offer any early bird bonus?

Yes, we will provide a 50% bonus tokens at the pre-sale phase. We will then provide a decreasing bonus structure at the token sale phase.

6 Do you have any Bounty Program?

Yes, we value your support and reward bounty AEN tokens for your efforts. Stay tuned for the announcement of the details on the Bounty Program.

7 When will I receive my AEN tokens?

AEN tokens will be allocated to you upon confirmation of contribution. Depending on the stage of contribution, there will be lock up restrictions on the withdrawal.
